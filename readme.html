**Freelancing Platform for SIH 2024** Project Concept This project aims to develop a comprehensive Freelancing Platform to bridge the gap between freelancers and employers seeking short-term and project-based job opportunities. The platform provides a robust job marketplace, profile management tools, AI-based recommendation systems, and secure escrow payment processing, making it easier for freelancers and employers to connect and collaborate effectively. Problem Statement There is a significant need to connect freelancers and gig workers with short-term and project-based job opportunities. Our platform addresses this gap by offering a seamless experience similar to Upwork, where freelancers can find work, and employers can find skilled freelancers for their projects. Proposed Solution Freelance Job Marketplace: A platform where freelancers can find short-term jobs and gig work while employers can post projects and invite applications. Freelancer Profile and Portfolio Management: Allows freelancers to showcase their skills, experience, and past work, along with a rating and review system. Extensive Search & Analytics: Enables extensive data search and generates AI-enabled insights for both employers and freelancers. AI-Based Recommendation Systems: Utilizes AI to recommend job opportunities to freelancers and suitable candidates to employers. Escrow Account Creation: Provides a secure payment mechanism, holding funds until project completion. Key Features AI Integration: Integrates third-party AI services and custom models to provide personalized job recommendations. Secure Payments with Escrow: Uses WooCommerce and custom plugins to facilitate secure escrow payments. Custom User Interfaces: Built with Elementor Pro to ensure a user-friendly experience. Scalable and Flexible: Built on WordPress, allowing easy customization and scalability. Technologies Used WordPress: CMS platform for building the freelancing platform. WooCommerce: For managing payments and transactions. Custom WordPress Plugins: For additional functionalities like escrow, messaging, and AI-based recommendations. AI and Machine Learning: Integrated through third-party APIs and custom models for recommendations. PHP, JavaScript, HTML/CSS: Core web technologies for customization and enhancements. Payment Gateways: PayPal and Stripe for secure transactions.
